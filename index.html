Project Monster

Card-based RTS/RPG

Click to get through the initial menus, then once at the map screen click a room on the first floor from the bottom. Click some more to get through the conversation controller, then use WASD to move.
You can hold the 'R' key to 'reload' your hand. When no cards are left in your Deck to draw from your discard pile will shuffle back into your deck pile, costing you time.
